Frequently asked questions

For clarity on the importance of terms and condition and privacy policy and how it can be generated, some frequently asked questions are answered below

The terms and conditions agreement is a website page or document that clarifies the rights, responsibilities, terms, conditions, and usages of anyone visiting a site. Essentially, it is a simple way to create a contract between the site owner and the site users. Terms and conditions usually feature an explanation of any key phrases used in the terms and conditions agreement, and an outline of the restrictions on the legal liability of the website owner incase of any damages incurred during use of the website. It will also include the website’s policy for any legal action that could be taken against any users that break the terms and provide users with legal advice as to the rights of the website owner to do so.

In case of legal dispute involving your website, product or service, the terms of this agreement will form the basis of your defense against any claim. Due to this, all websites, apps and online stores should create a terms of use agreement.
